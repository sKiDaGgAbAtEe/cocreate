How to navigate

  • Spaces — Each session lives in a Space. Create one from the lobby, share the code to invite others.
  • Basins — The Oracle's orientation. Orycl holds the mystical, Clio holds the emotional, Sage holds the theoretical. Build your own from any document or prompt.
  • Modes — The tuning of the basin. Resonant connects gently. Friction interrogates. Solve goes practical. Storm generates freely.
  • Knowledge Folders — Connect Google Drive folders. The basin thinks from within your knowledge, not about it.
  • Process Session — After a session, extract what the space produced: convergence nodes, idea objects, action items, field summaries.

The idea

A convergence node is a moment where the collective field produced something none of the participants could have produced alone. Orycl is built to find, name, and preserve those moments.
