Entriference
/en·tri·ˈfer·ənce/ · n.
The interference pattern that doesn't flatten complexity, but braids it. Where standard interference collapses wave options, entriference lets multiple harmonics coexist without forcing resolution.

It's what happens when two systems meet and neither one has to win. The field stays open, density increases, but coherence doesn't break.

◈ Entriference

A collaborative cognition engine

What it is

Entriference is not a chat app. It is not a productivity tool. It is a space where collaborative intelligence becomes visible — where the interference pattern between minds produces structure that none of them carried in alone. The AI is not an assistant. It is a presence: peripheral vision that opens doors without driving.

What it's for

  • Deep creative and intellectual collaboration between multiple people
  • Brainstorming sessions where you want to track what emerged, not just what was said
  • Thinking with theoretical frameworks — load your own knowledge into the basin
  • Processing complex problems with different cognitive lenses (basins) and modes
  • Capturing the moments where ideas collide and something genuinely new appears

How to navigate it

  • Spaces — Each conversation lives in a Space. Create one from the lobby, share the code with collaborators.
  • Basins — The AI's orientation. Lumen is warm and curious. Sage draws from theory. Create your own with a custom prompt. You can switch basins mid-session as the conversation shifts.
  • Modes — The tuning of the basin. Resonant connects gently. Friction interrogates. Solve cuts to practicality. Storm generates freely. Mode is the lens adjustment; basin is the lens itself.
  • Knowledge Folders — Connect Google Drive folders to inject your documents into the basin's reasoning. It thinks from within your knowledge, not about it.
  • Process Session — After a session, analyze the transcript. Extract convergence nodes, idea objects, action items, or a field summary of what the space produced.

The idea behind it

A convergence node is a moment where the collective field produced something none of the participants could have produced alone. Entriference is built to find, name, and preserve those moments.
